ENTREPRENAURSHIP COMPETITION

Entrepreneurial Competition was one of the different and exciting project created to challenge your creativity and business minds! Through 15 StartUp missions and customized teaching, you must come up with a business idea based on one of the following categories: GreenTech,service innovation,life tech and social entreprenaurship and our group chose life and green tech,after all the brainstorming and research our group came up with the idea to create pencils with implanted spores or orchid seeds, whereas the pencil is sharpened and the planted fiber and edible mushrooms would grow. However, since this idea was hard to execute we thought of changing our idea into something different and unique. We came up with the idea of making pencils out of paper and within that paper, the seeds would be implemented and that comes under life and green tech,moreover Our target group is mainly targeted among 30-60 year olds living either in Europe or the United States regardless of the gender. We chose our target group by researching orchid forums. We previously had some comprehension that people who grow orchids tend to be quite infatuated by them, thus we took to the internet to confirm this. Turned out that that was indeed true, orchid lovers would gather in various dedicated forums such as https://www.orchidsforum.com/forums/introduce-yourself.2/

I designed and created personas, logo and illustrations for this project from Photoshop and Illustraor and canva. This was a mandatory school project from first semester, where i was introduced to basic html,css,designing prototypes and wireframes ,on the other hand i was entitled what digital marketing and Usability test
